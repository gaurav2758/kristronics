About Kristronics

Kristronics Pvt Limited is an ISO 9001:2015 certified MSME registered with NSIC, GEM, Startupindia in Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, Ministry of Commerce & Industry Government of India having its R&D and Manufacturing setup at RAI Industrial Area ,Sonipat.With our latest Designs and Technology we are providing Various Solutions to Railways, Automotive and other Industries Like Integrated Passenger Information System (IPIS) for Stations, LED Displays and Video Walls (True Color Displays) for Railway Stations, Passenger Information and Infotainment System for TRAINS, LED Display System for BUS / Automotive Application, Intelligent Transport management System (ITMS), LED Based Coach Lights for Trains, Indoor & Outdoor Video walls for Advertisement & Information, Automatic & Manual Hand Sanitizer/Soap Dispenser etc. We have in-house R&D for Designing and Development & Testing of PCB’s, Circuit, Embedded, Software & Hardware etc.
