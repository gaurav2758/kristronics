About Kristronics

Kristronics Pvt Limited is an ISO 9001:2015 certified MSME registered with NSIC, GEM, DPIIT Recognized STARTUP Company by Ministry of Commerce & Industry Government of India having its R&D and Manufacturing setup at RAI Industrial Area ,Sonipat, Haryana. With our latest Designs and Technology we are providing Various Solutions to Railways, Automotive and other Industries Like Passenger Information and Infotainment System for TRAINS, Intelligent Transport management System (ITMS), Integrated Passenger Information System (IPIS) for Stations, LED Display System for BUS / Automotive Application, CCTV System for Trains and Buses, LED Displays and Video Walls (True Color Displays) for Railway Stations, LED Based Coach Lights for Trains, Indoor & Outdoor Video walls for Advertisement & Information.
