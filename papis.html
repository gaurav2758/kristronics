On Board PAPIS & Infotainment System

Enhancing Passenger Experience with Smart Connectivity & Safety

System Overview

The KPL On Board PAPIS (Passenger Announcement & Passenger Information System) is a state-of-the-art solution integrated with Infotainment capabilities for modern trains.

This GPS-based system ensures passengers are always informed about their journey status while enjoying high-speed connectivity and entertainment options. It combines visual displays, audio announcements, and safety features into a unified intelligent network.

GPS Enabled

Real-time location tracking for accurate next-stop announcements.

WiFi On Board

Seamless internet connectivity for passengers throughout the journey.

Key System Components

A comprehensive suite of hardware designed for the Indian Railways ecosystem.

Main Controller

The central processing unit that coordinates GPS data, audio announcements, and display outputs across the entire train. Rugged design for high vibration environments.

Exterior Front Display

High-visibility LED boards mounted on the train exterior (Head/Tail) to display Train Number, Name, and Destination (e.g., Borivali, Mumbai).

On Board PIS

Internal LED/LCD displays located within coaches to inform passengers of the current location, next station, and safety messages.

First AC PIS

Premium display units specifically designed for First AC coaches, offering enhanced aesthetics and clearer information visibility.

Toilet Emergency Alarm

Critical safety feature allowing passengers to alert the train crew from inside the washroom in case of a medical or security emergency.

Infotainment System

Delivers entertainment content, journey statistics, and internet connectivity directly to passengers' devices via the On-Board WiFi network.
