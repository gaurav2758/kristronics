Product Overview

Kristronics Bus Passenger Information Systems (PIS) are designed to perform reliably under continuous vibration, dust, humidity, and temperature extremes. They provide accurate real-time information to passengers and support fleet management operations effortlessly.

All systems conform to IS 16490, JnNURM, ARAI, and other Indian automotive standards. With modular components and robust electronics, Kristronics PIS ensures long-term reliability and minimal maintenance.
