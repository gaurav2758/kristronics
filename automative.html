Product Overview

Kristronics Bus Passenger Information Systems (PIS) are engineered to perform reliably in India’s diverse transport conditions—humidity, temperature variations, dust, and continuous vibration. The system provides real-time accurate audio-visual information to onboard passengers as well as at bus stops, enabling efficient fleet management.

Our LED displays, Driver Console Units, and supporting hardware comply with all major Indian automotive regulations and are built for long-term durability with minimal maintenance.

After Sales Service

Kristronics provides nationwide after-sales service through its dedicated support network. We maintain a high ratio of spare parts and trained field engineers, ensuring timely and professional service for all installed systems.

Driver Console Unit (DCU)

The Kristronics Driver Console Unit (DCU) manages route selection, real-time display updates, voice announcements, GPS tracking, and backend server communication. It features a user-friendly menu-driven system with USB-based updates.

DCU Features

  • Fully electronic, no maintenance
  • Menu-driven operation
  • High-brightness 16×2 LCD display
  • 4×4 tactile long-life keypad
  • Password protection
  • USB-based data updates
  • Lightweight & vibration-proof
  • Compliant with JnNURM, IS 16490 & ARAI standards
